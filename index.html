How to Play:

In each round, you alternate between taking your shot and dodging incoming fire.
The first to land 10 hits on their opponent emerges victorious!

Navigate the battlefield using the arrow keys (up, down, left, and right).
Strategically position your shots and hideaways with the keys q, w, e, a, s, d, z, x, and c.

Reset by hitting any key after a round.
